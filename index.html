About CMU-Africa

Pioneering engineering education in Africa since 2011

Carnegie Mellon University Africa represents a bold vision: bringing world-class engineering education to the heart of Africa. Located in Kigali, Rwanda, we offer the same rigorous academic standards as our Pittsburgh campus, while addressing the unique challenges and opportunities of the African continent.

Our programs combine theoretical excellence with practical application, preparing graduates to become leaders in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship across Africa and beyond.
